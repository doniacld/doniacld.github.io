The extended version of my TinyGo talk, presented at GopherCon UK 2022.

## Abstract

This longer format allows for a deeper exploration of TinyGo and the complete journey of building an IoT monitoring system:

- Introduction to TinyGo and its ecosystem
- Hardware selection and setup
- Working with temperature and humidity sensors
- Data collection and transmission
- Building dashboards with Prometheus and Grafana
- Lessons learned and tips for your own projects
