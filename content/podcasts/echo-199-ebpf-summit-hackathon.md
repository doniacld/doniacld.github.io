Episode 199 of the eBPF & Cilium Office Hours live stream (eCHO).

## About the Episode

Join Liz Rice and me as we reveal the winners of the eBPF Summit Hackathon, discuss submission highlights, and celebrate the community's contributions to the Cilium and eBPF ecosystem.

We were joined by special guests and Hackathon judges Constanze B. Roedig and Teodor J. Podobnik to talk through the judging process.

### Categories

We revealed the winners in each of the three categories:
- eBPF Starter Track for Beginners
- Cilium technologies
- Using eBPF
