## About the Episode

A crowd of blue gophers descended on the café for this episode! We're the co-authors of "Learn Go with Pocket-Sized Projects" and we discuss:

- What makes Go tick as a programming language
- Strange bugs we've encountered across our diverse experiences
- Our journey writing the book together
- Tips and insights for Go developers
