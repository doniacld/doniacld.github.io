## About WHAT06

I have been an active member of [WHAT06](https://what06.fr/) (Women Hackers Action Tank) since 2021. WHAT06 is an association dedicated to promoting women in tech in the French Riviera region.

## Contributions

### Events MC

- **[Hackathon Inclusion 2023](https://what06.fr/hackathon-inclusion-2023/)** - Hosted as MC a 24-hour hackathon focused on inclusion projects at SKEMA Business School, Sophia Antipolis

### Tech4Elles App

I am part of the team working on the [Tech4Elles](https://www.tech4elles.com/welcome) mentoring application, which connects mentors (marraines) with mentees (filleules) to help young women navigate their career paths in tech.

### WHAT06 Podcast Host

I host the [WHAT06 podcast](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eVMxXkdo6AU&list=PLm0sc9fbNh5R8wS3VGqbbmmldXvWR-DJ7), focusing for Season 1 on the Tech4Elles app. The podcast features interviews with experts and mentor-mentee pairs.

**My responsibilities include:**
- Sourcing and preparing episodes
- Recording in a professional studio
- Editing long formats and shorts
- Social media communication
