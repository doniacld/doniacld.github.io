Hello you,

My name is **Donia Chaiehloudj**. I am a software developer living and working in the south of France.

I am a **Golang and open-source enthusiast** striving to share my knowledge with others. I am a Google Developer Group organizer, Women Techmaker Ambassador, and part of a local women in tech organization called [WHAT06](https://what06.fr/).

## What I Do

- **Software Development**: Building applications with Go, focusing on clean code and practical solutions
- **TinyGo & IoT**: Exploring the world of microcontrollers and embedded systems with TinyGo
- **Public Speaking**: Sharing knowledge at conferences like GopherCon, ContainerDays, and meetups
- **Community Building**: Organizing tech events and fostering inclusive developer communities
- **Writing**: Co-authoring a book on Go and writing blog posts about my tech journey

## Focus Areas

- **Go & TinyGo**: From web services to microcontrollers
- **Cloud Native**: eBPF, Cilium, Network Policy, Kubernetes, and containers
- **Community**: Building and nurturing tech communities
